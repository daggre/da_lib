# Useful ptfx assets

## Looped assets
For slow bleeding effect that drops onto ground.
`scr_winter2 scr_blood_drips`

For faster bleeding effect that drops onto ground. *anm_blood has good effects
`anm_blood ent_anim_blood_drips`

Good blood spurt with pool (maybe useful for medrp or surgery?)
`scr_odr3 scr_odr3_blood_spurt`

Moonshine still sounds and smoke.
`core ent_amb_smoke_moon_still`

Wispy campfire smoke.
`core ent_amb_campfire_smoke_distance_trail`

Bee swarm for beehives or honey
`core ent_amb_insect_bee_swarm`

Liquid leaks that leave interactive puddles
`core liquid_leak_*`
- blood
- flour
- grain
- gunpowder
- kerosene
- moonshine
- oil
- petrol
- sand
- sugar
- water

Dynamite fuses
`des mob3 scr_mob3_proj_dynamite_trail` No audio
`scr_mudtown5 proj_dynamite_trail` Audio
`scr_winter4 proj_dynamite_trail`
`anm_weapons ent_anim_dynamite_fuse` Smoke no audio
`core proj_dynamite_fuse` Audio and smoke
`scr_net_dynamite proj_dynamite_trail` Audio and smoke
